Camp

ETM is a week-long summer camp for junior and middle school students focused on helping students enjoy the best environment possible to hear from and respond to Jesus. The week is packed with fun, friendship, and personal growth in Jesus Christ. Come “Experience the Mountain” in Welches, Oregon.

Gospel

At ETM we spend the week processing and responding to the gospel message about Jesus Christ and the relationship he is inviting us into with him. Our teaching, singing, discussions, prayer times, and forums are all focused around this goal.

Two Camps Together

Junior Camp & Middle School Camp run during the same times and stay mostly separate, but campers are brought together in very strategic times to give the opportunity for older students to serve younger students. Also, many upper grade high school aged staff work alongside adults serving as counselors and program staff.
